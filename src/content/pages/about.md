## Why we exist

Wellness talk gets loud: metrics before meaning, hustle before rest. We edit for **clarity** and **restraint**—and for the bridge between what research can say and what a life actually needs in the room.

**Who this is for:** people who read carefully, move between East and West without turning either into a slogan, and prefer an honest “we don’t know yet” to a loud promise.

We are not selling certainty. We offer **coherence**—language and pace you can try on a Tuesday that does not cooperate.

## How we work

- **Editorial first.** Essays and sessions that respect a finite attention span.
- **Small circles.** Community opens in waves; the room stays small on purpose.
- **Private work.** Consults and environment work move at a human cadence—integration, not intensity.
- **A narrow shelf.** Objects we would use at home. No trend velocity.

## Who we are

A small desk: writers, teachers, and designers with years in somatic practice, integrative health, and space. We sign our work. We fix errors in public when we must. Commerce stays second to trust.

## Colophon

*Wellness First Global* is set in quiet neutrals—meant to age, not chase a feed.
