**We are not a feed.** We publish at a pace that respects attention, and we edit for warmth without flattery. Long pieces earn their length; short ones earn their place.

## Evidence and story

We separate evidence from anecdote. Integrative thinking is welcome; borrowing the authority of a tradition to sell certainty is not. When we recommend a practice or object, we say **why it earned a place**—and when to leave it aside.

## Commerce

Editorial is never pay-to-play. The shop exists because some questions are answered better with a well-made object than with another app. When we mention a product, the reader should know if it is sold here—and **never** feel a hidden sell.

## Corrections

If we misstate a fact, we fix it plainly and note the change. If something feels off to you, write to the desk; we read every serious note. Good-faith correction is part of the contract.

## Care

We do not use crisis language to keep you clicking. If you are in acute distress, we point you toward clinical and emergency care—not because we are distant, but because the right help has to match the room you are in.
