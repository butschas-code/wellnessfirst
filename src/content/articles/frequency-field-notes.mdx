People use the word *frequency* to mean different things: a carrier signal, a habit of mind, a commercial product with a trademark. In this journal, we mean **carefully bounded support** for the body’s capacity to settle, repair, and attune—always secondary to sleep, relationship, and honest environment work.

The risk in this space is not curiosity; it is **category error**: buying a sense of progress when what you need is a quieter room, a fairer week, or a conversation you have been postponing.

> Tools belong in the margins of a life, not at the center of a personality.

## Where frequency fits in our map

We place frequency applications primarily under **regulation**—as optional aids for nervous-system load—and secondarily under **environment**, when light, material, or field conditions are genuinely part of the story. We are not a device site; we are an editorial house. If an object appears in the shop, it has to answer a human question with proportion, not hype.

When you are ready to sit with someone about how these pieces fit *your* context, the consult line is open. And if breath is the bridge you need first, there is a session for that too.
